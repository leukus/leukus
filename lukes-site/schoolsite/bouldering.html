Bouldering Club


The Bouldering Club is a fun way to spend your lunches. Many people tell youth to reach for the stars. In this case, the students are reaching for the top of the rock wall. inside the school. spending lunches climbing the wall is a source for entertainment and practice. Taking time moving from beginner to the hard routes, the students enjoy their time and can not get enough.
