Diversity Club


Here at MBSS, we have the Diversity Club run by Mrs. Keyworth and Mrs. Broadhurst. It is a group of students growing in size expressing their love and acceptance of everyone no matter what gender, expression religion, LGBTQ+, and. more. Anyone can come join the mix of diverse students in Mrs. Keyworth's class at lunch.
